Light Vision

Glow-in-the-dark additive
Light Vision from Zolatone is a photo-luminescent additive that can be mixed with Zolatone paints and seamlessly applied in corridors, around exits, in stairwells and to other obstacles. When the lights are on it has the classic good looks of Zolatone, if the power fails, Light Vision emits a soft glow that can help guide people safely toward the exits.

Finish Data

Brightness Rating Very High - 70 mcd (Millicandela)

Duration 20-25 minutes

Glow Color Green / Yellow

Daytime Color Same as specified finish

Charge Time 12+ minutes with fluorescent light
